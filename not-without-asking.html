Not Without Asking

Magic is life. The world is dark. Their love makes it worse.

Genre: Fiction / Fantasy Romance · Dark world, high stakes

Nessa West is a High Mage, brilliant and dangerous in ways the world pretends not to need. Nolan O’Connor is a magical vampire from an old bloodline, the kind of outcast people fear because it’s easier than admitting they want his power.

They should have never met. Now there’s no going back. The world is afraid, and it will stop at nothing to keep them apart.

Highlights

  • Forbidden love in a world where magic is survival.
  • Old bloodlines, political fear, and power that comes with consequences.
  • Romance with teeth: devotion, danger, and choices that cost.
