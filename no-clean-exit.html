No Clean Exit

Obsession, memory, and the cost of escape.

Genre: Fiction / Psychological

A dark psychological novel about obsession, memory, and the price of getting out. The kind of story where “starting over” sounds nice until you realize you brought yourself with you.

Highlights

  • Slow tension that tightens instead of exploding early.
  • Emotional stakes that don’t ask permission.
  • A protagonist trying to escape, while the past refuses to stay buried.
