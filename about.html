About

Who I am, minus the fake polish.

I write dark fiction with emotional teeth, and I keep faith honest. I’m interested in what people do when they’re cornered, what love costs, and what truth looks like when you stop performing it.

You’ll find stories here that don’t comfort on command. They ask better questions. If you’re the type who reads closely and thinks for yourself, you’re in the right place.
