The Memory That Killed Her

Genre: fiction / Mystery

Dark, unsettling, and emotionally ruthless, this standalone psychological domestic noir explores how power hides inside love, how systems reward obedience, and how survival sometimes requires choosing who gets to feel safe.

What readers will find inside:

  • Perfect for readers who crave slow-burn dread, unreliable narrators, and endings that refuse to comfort.
  • Once you start remembering with Morgan, there is no going back.
  • She was not only a victim. She was part of the violence.
